About #fstoco

#fstoco is put on by Full Stack Toronto, a non-profit organisation geared towards providing web professionals with intermediate and advanced educational resources. #fstoco is a unique conference because it covers all the layers of the web: Front-end, Back-end, DevOps, UX, project management, and product development. Questions? comms@fsto.co will respond as soon as possible. We do not provide visa assistance for attendees.

Watch the videos from 2015 to get a taste of the conference before you register!

Who Should Attend

If you work in the web industry #fstoco is for you! Every year, we bring together the world's brightest minds in the business to present on relevant topics with real-life applications. You will go back to work not only knowing how to do the latest and greatest, but most importantly the why.

Why You Should Attend

We believe that to become a better professional, you need to understand what the other members of your team are doing. We've planned our content schedule so that while you participate in your concentration/discipline you will also learn from other layers of the stack to further your career growth. All well being surrounded by your peers!
