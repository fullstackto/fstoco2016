About Full Stack Toronto

Full Stack Toronto is a non-profit organization that provides web developers with access to education and resources that cover intermediate to advanced web development, free of commercial content.

Speakers at Full Stack Toronto events are subject matter experts with extensive industry experience. Attendees will walk away having not only learned the why of what they're doing, but also how to execute on ideas.

Workshop-style sessions with a focus on all layers of web development—in a commercial free environment—is what really sets Full Stack Toronto events apart from all other development events.

Our Conference #fstoconf15

Full Stack Toronto Conference hosts speakers from around the world and across the stack! We organize things a bit differently too, all of our sessions are workshop-style where you can expect to leave with actionable items to continue your career growth, we keep the sessions free of commercial content so you don’t feel like you have been pitched to, and we leave ample time for you to network with your peers to learn about how they approach similar problems.

Our Content Focus

Show Us Your Stack

These talks are all about showing off a company’s development process. From the challenges they faced implementing their stack and how they overcame them. You will learn tips and tricks for using the same stack successfully.

Operational Layer

These talks are all about keeping everything running smoothly! The presentation and management layers. Topics include: continuous deployment, project management, user experience, web performance, web accessibility, web design, user experience, team building and more.

Programming Layer

These talks are all about getting everything to work! The application and data layers. These sessions cover topics like front-end development languages, back-end development languages, APIs, databases, big data, architecture and more.

Creative Code

These talks are for the hackers, inventors, tinkerers, and shift disturbers out there! In a non-commercial manner we’ll showcase how full stack is used to change everyday life, connect the hardware and software worlds, and other creative uses of full stack code.
