Our Programs

+= Workshops

Plus Equal Tech is our advanced workshop program. These are one day events held on Saturday's in downtown Toronto focusing on giving you a full-day of training on a particular web technology.

Visit Plus Equal

Monthly Meetups

Our monthly meetups consit of one or two talks often focused on telling a story about a technical journey. These are after work events to keep you up to date with what's going on in the development scene in Toronto.

Join our Meetup

Youth Program

Full Stack Toronto is happy to facilitate CoderDojo Programs across Canada and in Toronto. CoderDojo mentors teach kids to code with live code and industry software. Being a mentor is a great way to give back to the community.

Visit CoderDojo Toronto

Annual Conference

Every year we gather to bring together entire web teams (front-end, back-end, devops, and UX) to meet each other in an inclusive atmosphere for learning. Join us Oct. 23-24th for two days of amazing content and networking.
