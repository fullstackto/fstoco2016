Getting around Toronto

The host hotel is located right in the heart of downtown Toronto at 123 Queen Street West. It is accessible by public transit (Toronto Transit Commision - TTC [$3.25 cash fare]) with the 501 Queen Street Car and the Osgoode stop on the University-Spadina 1 line

From YYZ Pearson International airport you can take the UP Express [$12.00 cash or credit fare] to Union station and transfer to the 1 University-Spadina subway to Osgoode station [$3.25 cash fare].

From YTZ Billy Bishop Toronto City Center International airport you can take the Porter bus [free] to Union station and transfer to the 1 University-Spadina subway to Osgoode station [$3.25 cash fare].

The local transit cash card that works on both services and all routes is the Presto Card [$6.00 cash or credit for the card] you can add value to this card and recieve reduced fares, [$9.00 for Up Express, $2.90 for TTC] and you don't have to carry cash, just simply tap the card on the green buttons and go.

The host hotel is also connected to the underground PATH System that connects Union Station to the Eaton Centre and several office towers.
