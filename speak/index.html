Our Style

Are you ready for the challeng of speaking at Canada's most practical web professionals confernce? Yes? Good let's get started!

Speaking at Full Stack Toronso events is a unique experince. We ask for content that fits into our hybrid session model, free of commercial content, and relevant to current best practices and future ready!

Hybrid Session Model

To meet the hybid session model your talk must adhere to the following:

  • Be 30 minutes in lenght
  • Followed by 10 minutes of Q&A
  • Overview of a web concept
  • A deep dive into a part of that concept like a demo, code, or case study
  • Give attendees practical takeaways
  • Follow our Code of Conduct
  • Free of commercial content, no indepts of paid products or servicies, no promoting your employeer

What you get

All speakers at #fstoco will be paid a base honouraium of $1000CAD, we will then reimburse you for travel and lodging expenses in Canadian Dollars up to a certain amount based on your geographical region. Reimbursment will only happen if you book through our travel and lodging partners, some exceptions will be made for those that can't book with our partners. You must provide all recipts within 30 days of the end of the event to be reimbursed.

We are commited to increasing the diversity of our event and all tech events are offer a 10% bonus to the base compensation for those coming from typically underrepresented demographics.
